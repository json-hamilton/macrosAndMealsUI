About Shokuji

Shokuji is Japanese for "Meal", and that is the reason the website exists. Every fitness app was recommending us macros to help get rid of that last bit of belly fat, but there was nothing to help us hit these macros, without a great deal of effort on our side.

Shokuji was the answer. With this website, people at last have a central system to allow them to understand their macros but also help everyone achieve these with recommended meals based on each person's unique macro goal.
